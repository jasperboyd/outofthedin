About

Out of the Din is an independent and not-for-profit music reviewer, that focuses exclusively on independently released music. For many years record companies controlled what music made it out into the world. Nowadays thanks to the internet, record companies have become more of an obstacle in the creative process then a help.

Mission

The mission of this blog is to bring you reviews of independently released music. This means music released directly by the artist band or a not-for-profit record label.

We live in a world where musicians can create an album from start to finish entirely on their own. I believe firmly that artists create best when they have complete control over their process. Thanks to websites like Bandcamp and Soundcloud Musicians now can distribute their music on a global scale at no cost.
